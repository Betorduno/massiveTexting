Frequently Asked Questions

Some questions we often get asked are answered below!


How is this compliant? I thought permission was required...
Our attorneys have thoroughly reviewed MassiveTexting, and they believe the system is compliant as the laws are currently written for two reasons: 1. We are not selling or advertising any goods or services, so the vast majority of the law does not apply to our texts. 2. MassiveTexting is NOT an ATDS. Consent to contact is not required because MassiveTexting is not an auto-dialer.
Can I still get sued?
Absolutely. But we've done everything we can to mitigate that risk by pruning out litigious prospects before you message them, and we've built the system to be compliant with the law. We have never had a user penalized for using our system - has a 100% clean track record.
How much does it cost?
Your MassiveTexting membership will be charged on a subscription basis with renewals every 30 days. We have two subscription levels: Our Mid-Market plan is $500 a month, and our Large-Market plan is $1000 a month. To request access to MassiveTexting, schedule a time for a brief phone call at the top of this page.
What information will I receive from MassiveTexting's Skip Trace?
There is no other skip trace system that even comes close to providing as much value as this one. You'll receive: phone numbers, mailing address updates that we call the "golden address," email addresses, birth date, deceased flag, bankruptcy flag and relative's contact information!
